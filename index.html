Reading is so much fun!

Learn and have Fun!

Our classes are for children ages 4 to 12 years old. We make reading a fun group activity that your child will love. We have groups in Dublin, Cork and Galway. Our classes are designed to encourage every young reader to progress. Reading helps build independence and self-confidence and expands their vocabulary. Join us for a free taster session, just click on our Join a Club link.
